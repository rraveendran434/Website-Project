About Us

Mo's Pizza Shack was founded by Mo Mc Mo on Febuary 26th 1997. His idea when starting this restaurant was making a place where people can have a good time and eat great pizza. He wanted it to be the best New York pizza place. Throughout the years Mo has perfected his craft making many pizzas. Mo and his team have worked hard for many years to make sure the pizza he puts out is the best it can be. For most of Mo's life he has trained himself to make sure that what he puts out satisfies Him and his team at the restaurant know that when the food is served, you'll be 100% satisfied.
